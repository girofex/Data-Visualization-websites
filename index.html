Conflict and Human Suffering through Data

Project description

As war seems to be a constant in daily news and discussions, the public often loses sight of the real distribution of conflicts around the globe. ACLED (Armed Conflict Location & Event Data) is an independent, impartial global monitor that collects, analyses, and maps data on conflict and protest.

Through data visualization and interactive elements, we seek to provide a website which makes use of these data and renders them easily accessible and understandable to a wider audience.
